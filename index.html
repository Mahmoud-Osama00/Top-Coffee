Coffee

Coffee:
It is a stimulating beverage prepared by boiling or fermenting roasted and ground coffee beans. It is one of the most widely consumed beverages in the world and contains caffeine, which provides a feeling of alertness and energy.
Preparation methods vary across cultures, such as Arabic, Turkish, espresso, and French coffee.
Coffee:
The name given to the dried and processed fruits of the coffee tree, before they are roasted and ground into coffee. The origin of the word "coffee" is attributed to the Bon region in Ethiopia, where coffee is believed to have been first discovered.
So:
Coffee = raw beans (the source of coffee).
Coffee = the beverage resulting from coffee after roasting, grinding, and preparation.

cafe

Coffee is a widely popular global beverage. It is prepared from coffee beans picked from the coffee tree, dried, and roasted to varying degrees to yield diverse flavors and aromas. It is then ground and used to make a variety of beverages. Coffee is characterized by its unique taste and ability to energize the body and mind due to its caffeine content, making it a favorite choice for many to start their day or to recharge during work or study hours. Preparation methods vary from culture to culture, including Arabic coffee with its authentic flavor, Turkish coffee with its distinctive foam, strong Italian espresso, and modern varieties such as cappuccino and latte. Therefore, coffee is not just a beverage; it is part of social and cultural customs that reflect hospitality and communication between people around the world.
